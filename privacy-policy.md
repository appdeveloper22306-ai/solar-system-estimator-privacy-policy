## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Solar Estimator** ("the App") handles information when you use it.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This App is designed to function completely offline and does not collect personal data.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

The App does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

All information entered into the App, including:

* Monthly electricity usage (kWh)
* Backup hours
* Electricity rate (PKR per kWh)
* Selected solar panels, batteries, and inverters

is:

* Processed locally on your device
* Used only for performing solar system calculations
* Not transmitted to any server or third party
* Not linked to any account or identity

The App does not create user accounts.

---

## 3. Local Database Usage

The App includes a preloaded local database (SQLite using Android Room) containing:

* Solar panel specifications
* Battery specifications
* Inverter specifications

This database:

* Contains only product data
* Does not store user data
* Never leaves your device
* Is not accessible by the developer or any third party

---

## 4. Permissions

The App does not request or require access to sensitive device permissions, including:

* Camera
* Microphone
* Location
* Contacts
* Phone or SMS
* External storage

The App also does not require internet access to function.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

* Advertising SDKs
* Analytics tools
* Crash reporting services
* Cloud storage
* External APIs

No third-party services have access to user data because no user data is collected.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal data from any users, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for general audiences.

---

## 7. Data Security

Since the App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data, there is no risk of user information being accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed through our systems.

All calculations are performed locally on the device.
